Need some help? Yes. That's why you're here.

What am I doing here?

You're submitting your hours. This is basically a digital time-sheet. Enter your hours, get paid, drink a beer!

Why can't I submit my hours some other way?

This system is secure and accurate. This will not only keep your records visible to us, but also yourself. You can go back at any time and view your past work history with us.
This system also makes it easier for us to pay you, and creates less chance of error. Everybody likes getting paid!

Ok, so how do I submit my hours?

Well, since you can see this page, you're already logged in to the system. Everything you do from now on will be associated with your account. On your main homepage, you'll see the form. Your manager will help you with specifics, so contact them if you have any questions regarding what you need to write down.

How do I view me hours?

Under the form, you'll see your old entries. By default, this shows the last pay-period. If you want to see other pay-periods, select the *Calender/Icon/Button/Thing* and click the pay-period you'd like to view. For more details on a specific entry, just click the *Details/Button/More/Thing*.

What do I do after I submit?

I recommend a beer. Unless you're still in the middle of your work day, in which case you should contact your manager for a new task, and keep working. After you submit your entry, it will get automatically added to the database. No need to tell anyone - they'll be checking.
